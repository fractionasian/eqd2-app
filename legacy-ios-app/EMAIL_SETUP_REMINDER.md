## Quick Start: iCloud+ Custom Email Domain

1. **Subscribe to iCloud+** (if not already):
   - iPhone: Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí iCloud ‚Üí Upgrade to iCloud+
   - Choose 50GB plan ($0.99/month)

2. **Add your domain**:
   - Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí iCloud ‚Üí Custom Email Domain
   - Tap "Add a custom email domain"
   - Enter: peterbnguyen.com

3. **Verify domain ownership**:
   - Apple will provide DNS records (TXT, MX)
   - Add these to your domain registrar (where you bought peterbnguyen.com)
   - Common registrars: Namecheap, GoDaddy, Google Domains, Cloudflare

4. **Create email address**:
   - Once verified, create: contact@peterbnguyen.com
   - This becomes your primary or alias email

5. **Test**:
   - Send test email to contact@peterbnguyen.com
   - Check it arrives in your Mail app
   - Reply to verify sending works

**Total Time:** 10-15 minutes (plus DNS propagation: 1-24 hours)
