Privacy Policy for EQD2

Last Updated: January 17, 2025

Simple Summary: We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data. Everything happens on your device, and nothing leaves your device.

Overview

EQD2 ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data.

The App operates entirely on your device with the following characteristics:

What We DO NOT Collect:

  • ✗ Personal identification information
  • ✗ Health information or patient data
  • ✗ Location data
  • ✗ Device identifiers
  • ✗ Usage analytics
  • ✗ Crash reports with personal data
  • ✗ Any other user data

How the App Works:

  • ✓ All calculations are performed locally on your device
  • ✓ Calculation history is stored only on your device using iOS UserDefaults
  • ✓ No data is transmitted to external servers
  • ✓ No network connections are made
  • ✓ No third-party analytics services are used

Data Storage

Local Storage Only:

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Calculation history: Your past EQD2 calculations (up to 100 entries)
  • User preferences: Disclaimer acceptance status and last-used input values

This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not backed up to iCloud
  • Is deleted when you delete the app
  • Cannot be accessed by the developer or any third parties

Children's Privacy

The App is designed for medical professionals and is rated 17+ in the App Store. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Medical Disclaimer

This app is provided for educational and informational purposes only. It is not intended to:

  • Provide medical advice
  • Replace professional medical judgment
  • Be used as the sole basis for treatment planning decisions

All calculations must be independently verified by qualified medical professionals.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising frameworks.

Data Security

Since no data is transmitted from your device, there are no external data security concerns. Local data is protected by your device's built-in iOS security features (encryption, passcode/biometric protection).

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is no personal information to:

  • Access
  • Correct
  • Delete
  • Export
  • Object to processing

You have complete control over all app data by managing it directly on your device or by deleting the app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

International Users

The App can be used worldwide. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no cross-border data transfer concerns.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have specific rights. However, since we do not collect, sell, or share any personal information, CCPA obligations do not apply.

European Users (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), EU users have specific rights. However, since we do not process any personal data, GDPR obligations do not apply.
